Yujin Jeong

I am a PhD student at TU Darmstadt and a member of ELLIS program under the supervision of Anna Rohrbach (the Multiodal AI Lab, TU Darmstadt) and Iro Laina (VGG, University of Oxford). I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science at Korea University under the supervision of Jinkyu Kim. I had an amazing opportunity to complete an internship as part of the ML Research team at NAVER AI LAB, and I also visited the Scalable Trustworthy AI (STAI) group at the Tübingen AI Center as visiting researcher during my Master’s. Previously, I received my B.S. in Statistics at Ewha Womans University. My current research interest is 1. Compositional generative models (Diffusion mostly), 2. Analysis by Synthesis, and 3. Multimodal Generative models (Text-Vision-Audio).
