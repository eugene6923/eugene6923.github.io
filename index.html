Yujin Jeong

I am a PhD student at TU Darmstadt under the supervision of Prof. Anna Rohrbach in the Multiodal AI Lab starting from September 2024. I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science at Korea University under the supervision of Prof. Jinkyu Kim. I had an amazing opportunity to do an internship with the ML Research team at NAVER AI LAB. Previously, I received my B.S. in Statistics at Ewha Womans University. My current research interest is 1. Compositional Multimodal generative models (Diffusion mostly), 2. Interactive Generative-Discriminative VLMs.
